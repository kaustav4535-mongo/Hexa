Booking a Ride

Payment Lock System

Your payment is locked at booking time and only unlocks after a driver accepts. You will not be charged until a driver accepts and you complete payment.

Customer Conduct

6. Payments & Fares

All payments processed securely through Razorpay. We accept UPI, debit/credit cards, and net banking. We do not store card details — handled by Razorpay under PCI-DSS compliance.

Refunds

  • Driver cancels after payment: full refund processed
  • Cancel before driver accepts (locked): no charge
  • Cancel after payment: cancellation fee may apply
  • Refunds within 5–7 business days
