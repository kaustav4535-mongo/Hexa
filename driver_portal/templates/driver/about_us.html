Our Story
The Idea
Seeing the Gap in Local Mobility
Electric TukTuks everywhere in Assam — but no digital platform to book them. Customers couldn't track drivers, couldn't pay safely, and drivers had no professional system.
Architecture
3-Portal Flask System
Separate Flask apps for customers (5001), drivers (5002), and admins (5003) — all sharing one clean database module. Built entirely on Termux (Android).
Innovation
Payment Lock System
Your money is held until a driver accepts. No charge if no driver. Payment unlocks automatically — no page reload needed.
Tracking
Real GPS, Not Approximation
Removed IP-based location (showed wrong city). Switched to pure device GPS — pinpoint accuracy in Jorhat, Golaghat, Sivasagar, Dibrugarh, Tinsukia.
Today
A Complete Platform
Live GPS tracking, satellite maps, Razorpay payments, Google OAuth, English + Assamese + Hindi, admin approval workflows, and driver earnings management.
